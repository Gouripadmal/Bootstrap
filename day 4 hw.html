What is the difference between a road bike and a mountain bike?
A road bike is designed for riding on paved surfaces and has narrow tires, while a mountain bike is built for off-road terrain with wider, knobby tires.
What are the advantages of a mountain bike?
A mountain bike is designed for rough terrain, offering better grip, suspension, and durability on trails..
